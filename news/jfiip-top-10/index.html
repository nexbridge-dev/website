NexBridge Selected for JFIIP Best Innovative Startup Award — TOP 10

Visual JFIIP Best Innovative Startup Award · TOP 10 of 200+

Tokyo, February 24, 2026 — NexBridge K.K. has been selected for the Japan Fintech Innovation Initiative Program (JFIIP) Best Innovative Startup Award TOP 10, recognizing companies building infrastructure for Japan's next-generation financial system.

Recognized for HSM-First Stablecoin Infrastructure

JFIIP, a joint initiative between Japan's leading financial institutions and the Financial Services Agency, evaluated over 200 fintech startups. NexBridge was recognized for its uniquely Japan-focused, full-stack approach combining HSM custody, MultiSig key management, and a programmable policy engine into a single unified rail.

Selection criteria highlighted

  • Regulatory alignment with Japan's stablecoin framework
  • Institutional-grade custody architecture (HSM + MultiSig)
  • API-first integration for trust banks
  • Cross-border settlement potential beyond traditional SWIFT
"NexBridge represents the kind of bottom-up infrastructure innovation Japan needs to remain competitive in the global digital finance landscape."

What's Next

As a TOP 10 selection, NexBridge will participate in the JFIIP accelerator program through Q3 2026, working alongside trust banks, payment institutions, and the Financial Services Agency to refine its issuance and custody offerings for production deployment.
