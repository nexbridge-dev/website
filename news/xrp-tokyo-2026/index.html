NexBridge Exhibits at XRP Tokyo 2026

Visual NexBridge booth · Happo-en, Tokyo

NexBridge brought its enterprise-grade stablecoin infrastructure to Asia's largest XRPL-focused conference, hosted at the iconic Happo-en in Tokyo.

NexBridge participated as an exhibitor at XRP Tokyo 2026 — Asia's largest XRPL-focused conference — held on April 7, 2026 at the historic Happo-en in Tokyo. At the booth, NexBridge demonstrated its full-stack approach to regulated JPY stablecoin issuance, custody, transfer, and DeFi connectivity to institutional investors, developers, and ecosystem partners.

With XRP positioned as a title sponsor of TEAMZ Summit and the broader XRPL ecosystem gaining institutional traction in Japan, the event underscored the growing importance of XRPL infrastructure for stablecoin operations — a core focus area for NexBridge.

NexBridge On Stage

NexBridge co-founder Jean Zhu joined a panel discussion on Japan's XRPL ecosystem, sharing how full-stack stablecoin infrastructure — combining HSM-first custody, MultiSig key management, and policy-driven approval — can unlock institutional adoption from issuance through DeFi connectivity.

About XRP Tokyo 2026

XRP Tokyo 2026 is the inaugural edition of the largest XRPL-focused conference in Asia. The event brought together over 3,000 attendees from 50+ countries for a full day of keynotes, panel discussions, and networking at Happo-en in Tokyo.

The conference spotlighted innovations across the XRP Ledger ecosystem, with sessions on scaling the Internet of Value, stablecoins and tokenized deposits reshaping finance, institutional blockchain adoption in Japan and Asia, real-world asset (RWA) tokenization, and XRPL developer tooling.

About TEAMZ Web3/AI Summit

XRP Tokyo 2026 was held as part of the broader TEAMZ Web3/AI Summit 2026 — Japan's premier Web3 conference, attracting over 10,000 attendees annually and featuring more than 60 speakers from leading global Web3 and finance organizations.

A defining moment for Japan's XRPL ecosystem — and a platform for NexBridge to bring institutional-grade stablecoin infrastructure to the world.
